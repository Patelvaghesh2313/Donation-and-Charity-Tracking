How Our Portal Helps You ?

Current systems lack transparency in the field of Charity & Donations. In the case of transactions associated with Donations made to different Organization there's no proper maintenance of records and because of involvement of some corrupt peoples within the organization, has made people lose trust in this social cause.

The donor is unaware of whether their funds are being utilized properly or not. Corruption is the other reason that leads the donor to lose trust in charities. The proposed system helps social organizations to run various projects for social causes transparently without the involvement of third parties, using smart contract-based incentives which help to confirm their impact is verified without the interaction of third parties, and at the same time, it is accessible to everyone.

This makes it easy for donors, organizations, and other participating vendors to track their transactions and restore their trust in the commitment to such social organizations. The system will help build trust with donors, recipients, and other stakeholders involved in the process of Charity and ensure that the donation reaches the intended person while improving the total administration costs, speed, and efficiency.
